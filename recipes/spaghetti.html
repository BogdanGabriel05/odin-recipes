Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • spaghetti
  • eggs
  • Parmesan cheese
  • pancetta
  • black pepper
  • salt

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta
  2. Cook the pancetta
  3. Mix eggs and cheese
  4. Combine pasta and pancetta
  5. Add egg and cheese mixture
  6. Season with pepper and serve
